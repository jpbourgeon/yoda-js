Offline-first

Yoda stores your data in a local instance that works offline on the client, and can replicate to and from other instances.

Runs everywhere

Yoda's offers different implementations that behave the same on a single instance server, in a serverless context or in the browser.

Developer friendly

Yoda's API is meant to be familiar and consistent yet powerful and extensible.
